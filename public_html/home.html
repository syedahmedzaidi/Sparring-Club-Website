Welcome

The UCSC is geared towards promoting all styles of martial arts and providing a safe environment for members to develop their self-defence skills. The UCSC aims to increase members’ fitness and martial arts’ technique through light sparring and full body conditioning.

About Us

The UCSC – University of Calgary Sparring Club is the only Martial Arts club operating at the University of Calgary that promotes real self-defence. Led by a team of dedicated executives and martial arts fans, we aim to increase members’ fitness and martial arts’ technique through conditioning and light sparring. We are 100% devoted and focused on providing a positive and enjoyable experience while enforcing a safe learning environment where members can use their strengths and talents. We offer our members the freedom to choose and compete against their preferred opponent – regardless of size, weight, gender, and martial arts style. Since the club was founded in July 2015, it has amassed over 80 members (as of April 2016) with a strong sense of commitment. This club focuses on real-life sparring as opposed to traditional schools that teach only forms/katas to their members. In today's society people hardly receive any exposure to real-life combat, and therefore when the need finally arises, the average person is unable to protect himself/herself from threatening societal elements. By regularly engaging in sparring activities in a controlled environment, the UCSC aims to help its members increase their own strength, agility, technique and the most essential of them all - their self-confidence.
